MultilingProfiler creates vocabulary profiles of French, German and Spanish texts to help users assess the suitability of written texts and listening transcripts for target learner groups. Click on the MultilingProfiler tab or press the button below to try the tool.

Try MultilingProfiler Here

MultilingProfiler generates text profiles and statistical data using four list types (see here for more information).
  1. Customisable word frequency lists.
  2. Word lists aligning with the new GCSE.
  3. Cumulative weekly word lists aligned with the NCELP schemes of work.
  4. Custom lists.

MultilingProfiler allows users to directly edit texts in the profiler window and immediately profile their adapted texts.
